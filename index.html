Welcome!

I'm Haley, here I review recipes I found online (mostly Tasty recipes off Facebook). They make the recipes look relatively simple in the videos they post, and growing up without learning how to cook, it made me feel like I could be capable of making delicious food. I wanted to find a way to get involved with cooking while also sharing my experience with others. I hope that you find it helpful in creating your own tasty recipes if you decide to in the future :) enjoy!

Crock Pot Chicken & Dumplings

The overall prep time took around 4 and a half hours. The meal was relatively easy to prepare, and smelled really good the whole time it was cooking. It was 20 degrees Fahrenheit outside today so it was the perfect warm dish. Everyone enjoyed it, it tastes sort of like a homemade Chicken Pot Pie.

Unicorn Hot Chocolate

Very fun to make and looks really cute, pretty tasty. The whip cream with the lucky charms marshmellows were my favorite part. Personally I think it's too much work for a cup of hot chocolate, but would be fun to make with kids.

Salted S'mores Truffles

Super yummy! Great to snack on, very addictive. Also very simple and easy to make a lot that will last a few days. I recommend them!!

German Bread Bowl

Delicious! The sourcream mix with the onions were very good.

Slow-Cooker Cheesecake

I love cheesecake so personally I was very excited to try this. It was really good and easy, high recommended!
